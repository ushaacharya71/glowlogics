Accounting

Accounting is the systematic process of recording, summarizing, and analyzing financial transactions to provide information for decision-making. It involves tracking income, expenses, assets, and liabilities to give a clear picture of an organization's financial health. Core activities include bookkeeping, preparing financial statements, and ensuring compliance with regulatory standards. Accounting helps businesses and individuals plan budgets, assess performance, make strategic financial decisions, and meet legal requirements.
